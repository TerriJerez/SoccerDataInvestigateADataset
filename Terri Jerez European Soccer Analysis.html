Introduction¶

I will be analyzing the Soccer database which comes from Kaggle and which I accessed using DB Browser for SQLite. I reviewed the data and asked the following questions:

  1. Does the home team win more games than the away team?
  2. What is the goal difference?

To analyze these questions, I will research amount of games played and determine what percentage of the matches were home team wins, which were away team wins, and which matches ended in a tie. I will then look to see what the goal difference is between home and away teams.

The dependent variable in my project is a win. The independent variables are: home team, away team, and amount of goals.

Conclusion¶

In reviewing the data from the information I gatherered, there were more games won by the home team than the away team. 45.9% of wins were from the home team, 28.7% from the away team, and 25.4% that ended in a tie. In this case, "home court advantage" seems to be a definite advantage. Also, more goals were scored by the home team when compared to goals scored by the away team.

The cumulative home team goals were 14.2% more goals than the away team goals from matches during the time period. The correlation of home team goals compared to the total number of goals is .73, showing a strong positive correlation. The correlation of away team goals compared to the total number of goals is .63, showing a moderately positive correlation but not as strong as the home team goals. With this information we can conclude that it is more likely for the home team to score more goals than the away team. We can't conclude that being on the home team causes a win, but we can see that there is a correlation with the home team and number of goals scored. The away team also has a moderately positive correlation with the overall amount of goals scored but it is not as strong as the home team.

Limitations to the exploartion for this project include: The data was only anazlyzed from European teams indicating that the home court advantage may not always apply to other leagues in other countries. Also, it was anazlyzed over a certain time period and other time periods may not yield the same results. Also, we don't know how far each team had to travel each time they played, what the time difference between areas and countries was, or how much time the away team had to adjust.
